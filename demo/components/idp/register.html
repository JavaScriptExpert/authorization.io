Identity Provider: Registration

This is an account registration page for a simple demonstration Identity Provider (IdP).

An IdP would normally ask you for some basic information like a username, email address, or other details in order to create a local account. A demo username has been provided here. The IdP can then link the local account to a decentralized account by asking your browser to generate a decentralized identity.

Clicking the button below will create a new account with this demo IdP and then take you through the process to link it to a new decentralized identity.

Please enter a username to associated with your local account.

Username is required.

Username is required to be at least 3 characters.

Username must not be longer than 128 characters.
